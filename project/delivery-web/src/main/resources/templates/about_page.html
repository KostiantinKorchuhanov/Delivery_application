About FoodApp

How it Works

1. Order

Pick a restaurant, choose your favorite dishes, and confirm your order using your balance or bonuses.

2. Preparation

The restaurant receives your request and starts cooking. Once ready, it's assigned to a nearby driver.

3. Delivery

The driver picks up the food and brings it to your door. You can track the status in your order history.

💬 Have Questions?

For any questions, please feel free to contact us using the "Chat" button on the right bottom corner of the website. Our AI assistant is available 24/7 to help you with your orders or technical issues.
